KidFit - The App For Out Of School Physical Education

KidFit is an app created to facilitate physical education for kids of all ages. It connects teachers and students together through an interface of both activities and lessons. KidFit also has a competitive leaderboard functionality that puts each kid on a leaderboard to re-ignite the competitive spirit of physical education that is lost during times of the pandemic. KidFit uses a system of peer to peer tracking to keep each student accountable for the physical excercise they are assigned to keep each student happy and give each teacher enough time for lesson planning.

Emotion Determination Using ML - Using Neural Networks to determine emotions in audio

We trained 3 1D convolutional neural networks to determine the emotional state of a certain audio file. The 3 neural networks classified each audio file based on 3 seperate emotional indicators. These indicators were Valence, Approach motivation and Arousal. We then determined the emotional state of a speaker by evaulating the 3 emotional indicators and seeing where they best fit on a 3D plane.
